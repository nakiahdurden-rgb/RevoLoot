About RevoLoot

RevoLoot is a project for the 2026 the Website Design FBLA competition. This website will be presented at my state competition. I completed this project as a learning prerequisite for a passion project I have in mind. I wanted to test my skills in a real-world application.



What is RevoLoot?

RevoLoot is a student learning hub designed to provide an engaging and interactive experience for history students. I am a passionate lover of history and believe anyone can appreciate the importance of understanding our past. History is a beautiful amalgamation of the good and evil humanity is capable of. It shows up what makes us human.
